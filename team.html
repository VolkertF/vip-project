Meet the Team!

VIP is a collaboration between students from Hochschule Hannover and Grand Valley State University. We are a diverse group of students working together to create something exciting and useful. We are Fabian, Cyril, Alex, Gizem, and Johannes.


Fabian Volkert

  (fabian.volkert@stud.hs-hannover.de)

Hi, my name is Fabian!

I am in my seventh semester of applied computer science, where the first two were spent at the university of Hanover.I am, at this time, counting 21 years and came up with the initial idea behind VIP.

In my free time I consider myself a passionate gamer,movie watcher, and music listener, although I also really enjoy reading books,composing music and drawing, when I find time to do so. I go couple dancing on a regular basis and spend a lot of time volunteering as an EMT(Emergency Medical Technician) and group leader at the civil protection of the German Red Cross.

Technical spoken I have been working with C, C++, Java, Assembly, SQL so far and was appointed as a project leader two times in projects I worked on earlier in my study. I am not to fond of the idea of working on databases, as I understand the need for them, but don't really enjoy working on them. On the other side I am eager to learn more about managing a project in general and implementing the controls for the user to interact with the application.
